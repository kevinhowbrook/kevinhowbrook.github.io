All I have now is the spilling

of memories and pictures

rolling like piano keys down drenched stairs,

a noise in a door; the tips of your fingers.

I hope I see you, spinning your life,

like a web of forever around a tree.

I missed, I am missing, I am the longing

that you were beside me

I lost someone, and they lost me.

Now a chime from a flag pole

I am still here, a memorial of sea,

the freckles of your shoulders

A glistening pair of lips,

a boy (and a girl), kissed with eyes,

a birthmark like mine.

Tomorrow, there will be mist
